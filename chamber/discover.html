Plan to strengthen green spaces

Through this program, the Comprehensive Maintenance of more than 60 green spaces in La Matanza chamber of commerce is proposed in order to make them safer, more accessible and enjoyable for society. The tasks to be carried out include cleaning, hygiene, conservation and maintenance of both green areas and dry areas, playgrounds and kennels.
